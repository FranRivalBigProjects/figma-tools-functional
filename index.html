Frequently Asked Questions

What is a design system in Figma?

A design system is a collection of reusable components, styles, and guidelines used to maintain consistency across designs.

Why is component usage important?

Tracking usage helps identify inconsistencies, duplication, and unused components.

How can I optimize Figma exports?

Use proper formats, reduce file sizes, and avoid unnecessary layers or effects.

How do I maintain consistency in Figma?

Use shared components, styles, and enforce design system rules.

Why is my design system inconsistent?

Lack of governance, duplicated components, and inconsistent naming are common causes.
